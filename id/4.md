briz-zled (**BRIZZ**-uld), *adj*

Combination of the name "Brian" and the word "grizzled\_", coined
by [J.J. Geewax.][]

Also reminiscent of "drizzled\_", which is probably fairly
descriptive of how often I'll actually update this blog.
