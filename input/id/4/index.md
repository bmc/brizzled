briz-zled (**BRIZZ**-uld), *adj*

Combination of the name "Brian" and the word "grizzled", coined by
[J.J. Geewax.][]

Also reminiscent of "drizzled", which is probably fairly descriptive of how
often I'll actually update this blog.
